What's all this?

We all love the Golden Globe Race tracker, but sometimes you want to explore a boat's situation with the full functionality of windy.com. This page allows you to quickly open windy.com at the position of a boat taken from the Golden Globe Tracker."

For example in the picture on the left the bottom bar shows swell detail that simply isn't available on the Golden Globe Race tracker.

You can find the Lat/Long of a boat from the 'team dropdown' on the Golden Globe Race Live Tracker.

Paste the Lat/Long string from the boat e.g. "40° 30.78 S, 115° 11.07 E" into the Location box at the top of the page and a url will be created for you. It will open windy.com at the boat position you entered.
